guidelines
let's keep mist a heart-warming space
1. pour your heart out.
compliment a stranger’s smile, make that cute barista’s day, shoot your shot at the ra, or shoutout your friend's fit.
‍
mist is all about positive compliments. write about all the small things you appreciate in someone else! From their beautiful smile to their cute earrings, that person on the other side will be happy to hear your thoughts.
2. embrace your inner shakespeare.
Descriptive mists are more likely to turn into matches.
‍
If you’re interested, don’t skimp out on details! Talk about where you were, what you were doing, and how you bumped into your missed connection. Anything descriptive helps, have fun with it!
3. be respectful of others.
Avoid offensive or embarrassing language.
‍
Any rhetoric that attacks or targets someone else is not permitted; neither are explicit sexual references. Posts that are found in violation of this policy will be removed immediately, and the author banned.
4. flag problematic posts.
Help remove harmful content off Mist.
‍
You have a voice in this community. If you find any posts that you find objectionable, please consider flagging them. Flagged posts will automatically be sent to our team for review, and posts with a sufficient flag-to-like ratio will be deleted off our platform.
